📜 The VoidLight Server Rules

  • 👥 Be Respectful — No bullying, hate, or toxicity.
  • 🚫 No Spam — Avoid unnecessary pings or emoji floods.
  • 🧭 Use Channels Properly — Stick to topics.
  • ❌ Keep it Clean — No NSFW or disturbing content.
  • 📢 No Advertising — Don’t promote without permission.
  • 🛡️ Respect Staff — Don’t argue with staff.
  • 🙅‍♂️ No Begging — Don’t ask for roles/items.
  • 🧠 Use Common Sense — If unsure, don’t do it.
  • 🕵️ No Hacking/Exploiting — Fair play only.
  • 💬 Keep Conversations Friendly — No drama or toxicity.
  • 😄 Have Fun — Be part of the VoidLight vibe!
